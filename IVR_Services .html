What Are IVR Services Integrated with WhatsApp?

IVR (Interactive Voice Response) services integrated with WhatsApp allow businesses to streamline customer interactions by combining voice-based automated responses with WhatsApp messaging. This integration provides a seamless communication experience, enabling customers to interact through both voice and text channels. UrbanChat’s IVR services with WhatsApp integration offer a powerful solution for enhancing customer support and engagement.

How to Set Up IVR Services Integrated with WhatsApp Using UrbanChat

Sign Up with UrbanChat

Register with UrbanChat to access IVR and WhatsApp integration services. UrbanChat will provide guidance throughout the setup process.

Configure Your IVR System

Set up your IVR system by designing call menus, recording prompts, and defining call routing options. UrbanChat’s platform supports customizable IVR configurations.

Integrate with WhatsApp

Connect your IVR system with WhatsApp through UrbanChat’s integration tools. This allows you to send and receive WhatsApp messages as part of your IVR workflow.

Design Automated Responses

Create automated WhatsApp responses based on IVR interactions. For example, send appointment confirmations or follow-up messages after a customer call.

Monitor and Manage Interactions

Use UrbanChat’s unified dashboard to monitor and manage both IVR and WhatsApp interactions. Access real-time analytics and reports to track performance.

Optimize and Improve

Analyze interaction data to identify areas for improvement. Adjust IVR menus and WhatsApp messaging strategies to enhance customer experience and efficiency.

Ensure Compliance

Follow data protection and privacy regulations to ensure secure handling of customer information through both IVR and WhatsApp channels.
