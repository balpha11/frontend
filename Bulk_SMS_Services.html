Bulk SMS Services with UrbanChat: Efficient and Compliant Communication

What Is Bulk SMS Services?

Bulk SMS services allow businesses to send large volumes of text messages to multiple recipients simultaneously. This method is ideal for delivering promotional offers, notifications, alerts, and updates quickly and efficiently. UrbanChat’s bulk SMS services offer a reliable and scalable solution for managing your messaging needs while ensuring compliance with industry regulations.

Difference Between Marketing Messages and Transactional SMS

Marketing Messages

Purpose: Promotional content intended to drive sales or engagement.

Examples: Special offers, discounts, product announcements.

Regulations: Requires prior consent from recipients and must include opt-out options.

Transactional SMS

Purpose: Information related to a transaction or service request.

Examples: Order confirmations, delivery updates, account alerts.

Regulations: Generally permitted without prior consent but must be relevant to the recipient’s transaction or service request.

DLT Policies

Registration

Businesses must register on the Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) platform to send SMS, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements.

Message Templates

Obtain approval for message templates used in bulk SMS campaigns to avoid spamming and ensure compliance.

Sender ID

Use registered sender IDs to identify your messages clearly and ensure they are not blocked or marked as spam.

Compliance Reporting

Regularly update and report compliance information to the DLT platform to maintain messaging privileges.
