What Is Rich Communication Services (RCS)?

Rich Communication Services (RCS) is an advanced messaging protocol that extends the capabilities of traditional SMS and MMS, providing a richer and more interactive communication experience. RCS offers enhanced features such as multimedia messaging, group chat, read receipts, and interactive elements like carousels and quick replies. UrbanChat enables businesses to leverage RCS to improve customer engagement and deliver a more dynamic messaging experience.
