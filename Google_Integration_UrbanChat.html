Google Sheets Integration with UrbanChat

Google Sheets integration with UrbanChat allows you to connect your chatbot and communication systems directly with Google Sheets. This integration enables seamless data synchronization, reporting, and management by automatically updating your spreadsheets with information from your chat interactions, leads, and other business processes.

What It Is

Google Sheets integration with UrbanChat allows you to connect your chatbot and communication systems directly with Google Sheets. This integration enables seamless data synchronization, reporting, and management by automatically updating your spreadsheets with information from your chat interactions, leads, and other business processes.

How to Set It Up Using UrbanChat

Obtain Google Sheets API Credentials

Go to the Google Cloud Console. Enable the Google Sheets API. Create credentials (OAuth 2.0 Client ID) and download the JSON file containing your credentials.

Access UrbanChat Dashboard

Log in to your UrbanChat account. Navigate to the integration section in the settings menu.

Choose Google Sheets Integration

Select Google Sheets from the list of available integrations.

Authenticate and Configure

Upload the credentials JSON file or enter your OAuth 2.0 Client ID and secret. Authorize UrbanChat to access your Google Sheets account.

Set Up Data Sync

Choose the specific Google Sheets file and worksheet where you want to store or retrieve data. Define the data mapping between UrbanChat fields and the columns in your Google Sheets.

Configure Triggers and Actions

Set up triggers in UrbanChat to automatically update your Google Sheets based on specific actions or events (e.g., new lead entry, chat transcript).

Test the Integration

Run tests to ensure that data is correctly synced between UrbanChat and Google Sheets. Verify that updates are accurate and timely.

Monitor and Optimize

Regularly check the integration for any issues or errors. Optimize settings as needed to ensure smooth operation.
